## Overview

At AutoFlow, I spearheaded the development of an innovative e-commerce platform tailored for car dealerships. The project aimed to not only showcase vehicles with high-quality visuals but also streamline the complex process of inventory management, making operations more efficient and user-centric.

## Key Features

- **AI-driven Image Enhancement:**
  Integrated advanced AI tools to automatically remove backgrounds and enhance images with artificial shadows, significantly boosting the visual appeal of vehicle listings.
- **Robust Backend Infrastructure:**
  Developed a secure and scalable backend using Django and FastAPI, ensuring reliable processing and management of extensive inventory data.
- **User-friendly Interface:**
  Built a responsive front-end with React that delivers a seamless browsing experience for both dealership staff and potential buyers.

## Technologies Used

- **Backend:** Django, FastAPI
- **Frontend:** React
- **AI Integration:** Custom solutions for automated image enhancement
- **Cloud & DevOps:** Modern deployment strategies to ensure high availability and performance

The platform has successfully transformed how car dealerships manage their inventories by:
- Reducing manual effort through automation.
- Enhancing the visual quality of listings, leading to higher customer engagement.
